Penalty Kicks

The team awarded the 'Penalty kick' has a chance to take a shot on goal from 12 yards, striker vs goalkeeper, with all other players having to stay outside the box until the ball is struck.

Awarded For
It is awarded when an offence punishable by a direct free kick is committed by a player in their own penalty area.
Referee Signals
Whistle blown long and firm
Points directly to the penalty spot of the team
What happens
The ball is placed on the penalty mark, regardless of where in the penalty area the foul occurred.
A player is allowed to take a single shot at the goal while it is defended only by the opposing team's goalkeeper.

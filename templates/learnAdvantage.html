Advantage

Common scenarios

An advantage is given when a foul and stoppage in play would actually be harmful to the offended team, as determined by the discretion of the official. The official is permitted to disregard the foul and let the game continue.

Hand Signals

  • Both arms put out parallelly
  • Pointing to the goal of the team that has the advantage.
Referee does not blow the whistle for this signal.

Red Card

The player is ejected from the field and is allowed no further part in the game.

Examples of offences
One player punching another player
Severe offences or for multiple yellow cards
Referee Signals
Point the red card at them and then hold it straight up in the air, just like with a yellow card.
If the ref gives the player a red card for receiving 2 yellow cards, the ref will point the yellow card at them first, followed by the red card.

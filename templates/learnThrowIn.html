Throw-In

A method of restarting play in a game of association football when the ball has exited the side of the field of play.


Common scenarios
A throw-in is awarded to the opponents of the player who last touched the ball when the whole of the ball passes over the touchline, on the ground or in the air.
Referee Signals
Points in the direction the throw is to be taken
The team attacking in the direction they are pointing takes the throw.

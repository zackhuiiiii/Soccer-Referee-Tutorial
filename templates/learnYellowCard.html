Yellow Card

Considered as a warning. Two yellow cards equal to one red card

Examples of offences
A harsh tackle where the tackler didn't make any contact with the ball
Unsportsmanlike behavior
Delaying the restart of the play or persistent minor offences
Referee Signals
Takes the card out of their pocket, points it at the player, then points it in the air.
Writes the details of the offence in their notebook.

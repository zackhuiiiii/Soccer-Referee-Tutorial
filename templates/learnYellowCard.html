Yellow Card

Considered as a warning Two yellow card equals to one red card
Referee Action:
Takes the card out of their pocket, points it at the player, then points it in the air. Write the details of the offence in their notebook.
Examples of offences:
A harsh tackle where the tackler didn't make any contact with the ball
unsportsmanlike behavior Delaying the restart of the play or persistent minor offences
